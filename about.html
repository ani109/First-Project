About Founder

SUDHA SINGH

Sudha singh hails from Uttarpradesh.Few summers Back She came to Mumbai To pursue her dancing career.She is and Accomplish Kathak Dancer.Being and artish one has and soft and sensitive heart too

looking at the less privilaged people around,her went out tohelp them.She had only one means to generate funds for carrying out the charity i.e music,dance and Art. Keeping charity in mind she started a trust in the name of ALANKAR CREATION PUBLIC CHARITABLE TRUST IN THE YEAR 1992

She immediatly started helping the fellow musician who werw physically unable to earn their livehood and critically ill by supporting them financialy.Keeping charity in mind ,She joined BJP in 1998 and now she is the president of the MAHILA MORCHA,Andheri Wing

Smt. SUDHA SING has now expanded her area of work by looking after the women empowerment subject and betibachao and betipadhao movement. Under this scheme she has distributed sewing machines to womwn of low income slab and also adopted 3 girl child,to look after their every need. This is specially to stop the rate of female foeticide in india. Before the entire womwn species gets extinct fron india,its high time to look in to the ratio of male and female.

Government of india has recognised her trust as An Institute of Arts,Culture and Music, Registerd Under Public Trust Act,1950,Regd.No F-26190

Vision

Alankar support Arts and Culture and works with economically weaken Sections of the Society and its Mission is to : To help the fellow musicians who were physically unable to earn their livelihood or critically ill by supporting them financially . Facilitate livelihood for the women from slums and rurals Villages. Inclate healthy and hygienic habits among slums and rurals communties . Educate less privileged children with the aim of imparting life skills .
